Abandoned Restaurants

Lankershim Blvd

A photographic selection of five abandoned buildings, each being an economic and geographic representation of its place along Lankershim Blvd.

In the hopes of highlighting a recurring issue, Abandoned Restaurants acts as a photographic catalogue of independently owned restaurants that have ceased to operate because of various issues that haunt local business owners in today’s economy. From Film Industry buyouts and downsizing, to violent crime, a pattern of failure may be observed. Based on hard data surrounding these locations, it is ultimately up to the viewer to associate observation with effect.
